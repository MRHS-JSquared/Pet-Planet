# Pet Planet - Quick Reference Card

## Essential Information

### Time System
- **1 game day** = 2 real minutes
- **Daytime**: 7 AM - 8 PM (13 hours)
- **Nighttime**: 8 PM - 6 AM (10 hours)
- **Skip Day**: Only available 8 PM - 6 AM, jumps to 7 AM

### Starting Conditions
- **Budget**: $100
- **Pet Stats**: All start 80-100%
- **Location**: Day 1, 7:00 AM

### Action Costs
- **Feed**: $5
- **Rest**: Free
- **Treat**: $8
- **Clean**: $4 (once per day)
- **Vet**: $25 (once per day)
- **Sleep**: Free (night only)

### Earning Money
- **Wash Dishes**: $10
- **Vacuum Room**: $15
- **Do Laundry**: $12
- **Homework**: $20
- **Yard Work**: $25
- **Organize Closet**: $18

### Pet Stats (0-100%)
- **Hunger**: Decreased by time, play. Increased by feed, treat
- **Happiness**: Decreased by time. Increased by play, clean, treat
- **Health**: Decreased by time, low hunger. Increased by feed, rest, vet
- **Energy**: Decreased by play. Increased by rest, sleep
- **Hygiene**: Decreased by time. Increased by clean

### Achievements
1. **First Week**: Survive 7 days
2. **Pet Master**: Reach level 10
3. **Financial Master**: Earn $500 total
4. **Pet's Best Friend**: 80+ happiness for 3 days
5. **Clean Freak**: Clean 20 times
6. **Veterinarian**: 10 vet visits
7. **Treat Giver**: 50 treats
8. **Night Owl**: 30 sleeps
9. **Marathon**: Survive 30 days
10. **Legend**: Level 50 + $1000

### Key Features
- 3D pet playground with interactive toys
- Financial analytics with charts
- Achievement notifications
- Day/night visual cycle
- Local storage persistence

### Educational Goals
1. Budget management
2. Income vs expenses
3. Opportunity cost
4. Emergency funds
5. Responsibility
6. Planning ahead

## Demo Tips

### 2-Minute Demo Path
1. **0:00** - Show home page, explain mission
2. **0:20** - Create pet, show setup
3. **0:40** - Perform actions, show costs
4. **1:00** - Complete chore, earn money
5. **1:20** - Show analytics, achievements
6. **1:40** - Demonstrate day/night cycle
7. **2:00** - Wrap up key features

### Questions to Anticipate
**Q: How does this teach finance?**
A: Students manage a budget, track expenses, make spending decisions, and see consequences of poor financial management.

**Q: What makes this educational?**
A: Clear financial concepts (income, expenses, budgeting), visual analytics showing spending patterns, and real consequences for poor decisions.

**Q: Why 2-minute days?**
A: Allows quick demonstration and testing. Can be easily adjusted for longer gameplay.

**Q: What technologies did you use?**
A: Next.js, TypeScript, Three.js for 3D, Recharts for analytics, Tailwind CSS for styling.

## Common Issues & Solutions

**Issue**: Skip day not showing
**Solution**: Wait until 8 PM (night time)

**Issue**: Can't do chore again
**Solution**: Wait for next game day (daily limit)

**Issue**: Pet died
**Solution**: Click "Start Over" - teaches consequences

**Issue**: Out of money
**Solution**: Complete more chores to earn money
